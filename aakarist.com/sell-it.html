About the Project

Sell It brings us a platform to sell pre owned electronics. They purchase used cell phones, tablets and even watches. Accepting the devices even with a crack adds a silver lining and sets their brand apart. With a vision to serve their customers best, they accept all brands ranging from Apple to Samsung. Offering the best deals at your comfort, Sell It is one of the fastest growing companies in its own field.

Challenges

Our client planned to step in an emerging industry comprising of a number of established competitors. Operating business offline has its own shortcomings. On realising this, our client wished to bring their business online in order to enable themselves to work on a larger scale. They were well aware of the significance of branding in today’s era and hence approached us for their logo designing. Once satisfied with the logo, they trusted us with their website development and digital marketing campaigns as well. Industries like these are one of a kind. Highly fluctuating screen prices and rapid upgradations makes the website management exhaustive. Therefore, developing a handy website emerged as the immediate need.

Solutions

In order to resolve the issues, we created a special dashboard for the client wherein the calculations could be done on percentage basis, eliminating the flat rate concept.

On our recommendation, the client agreed to develop two different applications for customers and vendors. We built mobile applications for both Android and IOS users. The application for customers was named Sell It and the other was named Logistics.

We worked on different ad campaigns on various social media platforms. Along with that, we laid down a blog marketing strategy and worked on off page SEO and on page SEO. A promotional video was also prepared for their YouTube channel. Also, to mark their presence online we handled their social media and converted likes to sales.

Results

With dedication and continuous efforts of all our team members, we could create easy to use website and application. When on one side the website marked their online presence, application allowed speedy access to the users on the other. This further enhanced customer satisfaction. Our logo was exuberant and impressionable.
